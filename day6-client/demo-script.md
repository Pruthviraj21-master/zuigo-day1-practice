# CreatorBridge Client Demo Script

## Introduction

### What to say:

"Welcome to CreatorBridge. This platform helps businesses and creators discover each other, create collaboration opportunities, and manage partnerships through a simple workflow.

Today I will demonstrate the complete collaboration journey, starting from discovering the platform, creating a business request, creator application, reviewing applicants, and managing collaborations through dashboards."

### Value Point:

CreatorBridge simplifies the creator-brand collaboration process by bringing discovery, applications, and management into one place.

---

# Step 1: Landing Page

## Screen:
CreatorBridge Homepage

## What to say:

"This is the CreatorBridge landing page.

It introduces the platform and explains how businesses and creators can benefit from using it.

Businesses can understand how they can find creators for campaigns, while creators can understand how they can discover new collaboration opportunities."

## Actions:

- Show homepage
- Explain creator and business benefits
- Navigate to registration/login

## Value Point:

"Instead of businesses manually searching for creators and creators waiting for opportunities, CreatorBridge creates a dedicated marketplace where both sides can connect."

---

# Step 2: Business Creates Collaboration Request

## Screen:
Business Dashboard → Create Collaboration Request

## What to say:

"Now we move to the business workflow.

A business can create a collaboration request by providing campaign information such as:

- Campaign title
- Budget
- Preferred platform
- Location
- Requirements

After publishing, the opportunity becomes available for suitable creators."

## Actions:

- Open business dashboard
- Select create campaign
- Enter campaign details
- Publish request

## Value Point:

"Businesses save time because they can clearly communicate their requirements and allow suitable creators to apply directly."

---

# Step 3: Creator Discovers and Applies

## Screen:
Creator Dashboard → Opportunity Discovery

## What to say:

"Now we switch to the creator side.

Creators can browse available campaigns and find opportunities matching their interests, category, and content style.

Once they find a suitable opportunity, they can submit an application."

## Actions:

- Open creator dashboard
- Browse campaigns
- Open campaign details
- Apply

## Value Point:

"Creators get easier access to collaboration opportunities without needing to search for brands individually."

---

# Step 4: Business Reviews Applicants

## Screen:
Business Dashboard → Applications

## What to say:

"After creators apply, businesses can review applicants from their dashboard.

They can view creator information and decide which creators are the best fit for their campaign."

## Actions:

- View applicants
- Open creator profiles
- Review applications
- Update collaboration status

## Value Point:

"Businesses get an organized process for managing creator applications instead of handling multiple conversations separately."

---

# Step 5: Dashboard Summary

## Screen:
Creator Dashboard / Business Dashboard

## What to say:

"Both creators and businesses have dashboards to track their activities.

Businesses can monitor campaigns, applications, and collaboration progress.

Creators can track their applications and active collaborations."

## Actions:

- Show dashboard overview
- Show active collaborations
- Show status tracking

## Value Point:

"The dashboard keeps the complete collaboration lifecycle organized from discovery to completion."

---

# Closing

## What to say:

"CreatorBridge provides a structured way for creators and businesses to discover, connect, and collaborate.

This MVP creates the foundation for future improvements such as AI recommendations, analytics, and additional collaboration tools."

## Value Point:

"The platform reduces manual effort and creates a smoother collaboration experience for both creators and businesses."
