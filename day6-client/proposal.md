# CreatorBridge Client Proposal

## Problem

Independent creators and brands often struggle to find the right collaboration partners.

Creators with small and medium audiences may have difficulty discovering suitable brand opportunities, while businesses spend significant time manually searching for creators who match their audience, niche, content style, and campaign requirements.

The current collaboration process is often unorganized, requiring businesses and creators to depend on multiple platforms, messages, and manual communication methods.

## Proposed Solution

CreatorBridge is a platform that connects creators and businesses through a simple collaboration workflow.

The platform allows businesses to discover suitable creators, publish collaboration opportunities, and manage applications.

Creators can create profiles, discover relevant brand opportunities, apply for campaigns, and track their collaboration progress.

CreatorBridge creates a structured environment where businesses can find suitable creators faster and creators can access more relevant opportunities.

## Scope (Included in This Version)

The MVP version includes:

### Creator Features

- Creator profile creation
- Creator information management
- Display of creator category/niche
- Opportunity discovery
- Campaign application system
- Collaboration tracking dashboard

### Business Features

- Business profile creation
- Business information management
- Collaboration request creation
- Campaign listing management
- Reviewing creator applications
- Collaboration status tracking dashboard

### Platform Features

- Creator and business discovery
- Category-based matching
- Basic messaging between creators and businesses
- Campaign listing and application workflow
- Dashboard for managing collaborations

## Assumptions

This estimate depends on the following assumptions:

- The first release focuses only on the MVP workflow.
- Advanced AI recommendations are not included.
- Payment processing is not included.
- Social media platform integrations are not included.
- Users will manually provide profile and campaign information.
- Requirements remain stable during development.
- Feedback and approvals are provided within reasonable timelines.
- The priority is launching a usable product rather than building every possible feature.
