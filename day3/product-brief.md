CreatorBridge Product Brief
Problem

Independent creators and brands struggle to discover, connect, and collaborate with the right partners. Creators often lack access to suitable brand opportunities, while companies spend significant time searching for creators who match their audience, niche, and campaign goals.

Value Proposition

CreatorBridge is a platform that connects creators and brands through a simple discovery and collaboration system. It helps creators find relevant opportunities and enables brands to identify suitable creators faster using profile information, audience data, and content categories.

Target Personas

1. Emerging Creators

Creators with small to medium audiences who want brand collaborations, growth opportunities, and better visibility.

2. Brands and Marketing Teams

Companies looking for relevant creators to promote products and reach specific audiences efficiently.

3. Creator Agencies / Managers

Professionals managing multiple creators who need tools to discover opportunities and organize partnerships.

MVP Scope

In Scope:

Creator profile creation
Brand profile creation
Creator discovery and search
Category/niche-based matching
Collaboration request system
Basic messaging between creators and brands
Campaign listing and application flow
Dashboard for managing collaborations
Out of Scope (v1)
Advanced AI creator recommendations
Automated contract generation
Payment processing
Detailed analytics dashboards
Social media platform API integrations
Creator verification system
Multi-language support
Competitor Landscape

Existing platforms like Upfluence and Aspire focus on influencer discovery and brand collaboration, mainly targeting established businesses. CreatorBridge differentiates by focusing on a simpler, accessible collaboration experience for emerging creators and smaller brands.
